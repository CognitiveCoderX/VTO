Orders & Shipping

How do I track my order? expand_more

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status by logging into your account and viewing your order history. Each order will have a tracking link that will take you to the carrier's website for detailed delivery information.

How long does shipping take? expand_more

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days after production is complete. Express shipping options are available at checkout for 2-3 business day delivery. International shipping may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination country.

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order? expand_more

Address changes can only be made within 24 hours of placing your order and before it enters production. Please contact our customer service team immediately if you need to change your shipping address.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? expand_more

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for standard products. Custom designed items cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect or quality issue. All returns must be in original condition with tags attached.

How do I request a refund? expand_more

To request a refund, log into your account, go to your orders, and select "Request Return" for the applicable order. Follow the instructions to print a return label. Once we receive and inspect your return, a refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Design & Customization

How does the 3D visualization work? expand_more

Our 3D visualization tool uses advanced WebGL technology to render your custom designs in real-time. It allows you to see how your design will look on different products from various angles. The visualization is an approximate representation of the final product, with actual colors potentially varying slightly from what's shown on screen.

What file formats do you accept for uploads? expand_more

We accept PNG, JPG, SVG, AI, and PSD files for custom designs. For best results, vector formats (SVG, AI) are recommended, especially for designs with text or logos that need to be scaled. All images should be at least 300 DPI for optimal print quality.

Can I save my design and come back later? expand_more

Yes, you can save your designs to your account at any point during the design process. Simply click the "Save Design" button in the customization tool. You can access all your saved designs from your account dashboard under "Your Designs" and continue editing them at any time.

Technical Support

The 3D visualization isn't loading. What should I do? expand_more

If the 3D visualization isn't loading, try these steps:
1. Ensure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
2. Enable WebGL in your browser settings
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Disable browser extensions that might be interfering
5. Check that your device meets the minimum system requirements
If the issue persists, please contact our technical support team.

How do I enable my camera for the virtual try-on feature? expand_more

To use the virtual try-on feature, your browser will request permission to access your camera. When prompted, select "Allow." If you previously denied access, you'll need to update your browser permissions. In Chrome, click the camera icon in the address bar and select "Always allow." In other browsers, check your site settings or privacy settings to enable camera access for our site.
